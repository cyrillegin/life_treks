Hi, I'm Cyrille

I'm a software engineer, inventor, beer brewer, and avid outdoorsman. I work for a start-up as their lead front-end engineer. I like playing with IoT systems and creating home automation systems. I'm also in the final stretch of a computer science degree, I already have an animation/visual effects bachelors.

It all started when I got my goldfish... I wanted to make sure their temperature was perfect. I got myself an Arduino, hooked up a temperature sensor, and watched the readings come it. Years of development later, I now run a custom dashboard giving me real time updates from the temperature out on my balcony, the moisture of the soil for Earl (he's a begonia) to if there's any motion in my living room when there shouldn't be.

Lately my interests have been in home efficiency and automation. One of my latest projects has been creating a temperature regulated fermentation fridge. Next up will be creating an auto-waterer for Earl. The goal of all these little projects is to start creating a repository for these simple easy to do projects that anyone at any experience level can setup.
